The challenge

Ulcerative colitis is a chronic inflammatory bowel disease affecting millions worldwide. Current treatments suppress the immune system to reduce inflammation, but do not directly promote repair of the intestinal lining — even though mucosal healing is the key predictor of long-term remission. Growing evidence points to the gut microbiome, and in particular depleted levels of secondary bile acids, as a core driver of the disease.

Our approach

Eleum is developing a Live Biotherapeutic Product — defined strains of beneficial bacteria engineered to restore secondary bile acid production, rebalance bile acid signalling, and accelerate healing of the intestinal lining. Rather than targeting the immune system, Eleum's therapy works with the body's own repair processes to strengthen the gut barrier and pursue long-term remission.
