The Science

Bile acids are molecular
messengers. We listen.

Produced at the intersection of the liver, the gut, and the microbiome, bile acids are far more than digestive molecules. They are signalling agents — regulating inflammation, shaping tissue repair, and influencing metabolic homeostasis across multiple organ systems.

Their disruption is implicated in a growing number of diseases. Eleum is building the scientific and technological foundation to understand, measure, and harness this biology — at scale, and across the full breadth of what it can offer.

Our Product

A Live Biotherapeutic Product
in preclinical development.

Eleum's lead programme is a Live Biotherapeutic Product — a defined consortium of beneficial bacteria designed to restore secondary bile acid production and actively promote mucosal healing. Unlike conventional probiotics, LBPs are developed as medicines: formulated, characterised, and evaluated against specific disease targets.

Indication

A clear first target.
A broader horizon.

Bile acid dysregulation is a driver across a spectrum of conditions. Eleum's initial focus is where the science is most mature and the unmet need is greatest.

Lead indication
Ulcerative Colitis
Chronic inflammatory bowel disease

UC causes persistent inflammation and ulceration of the colon, leading to abdominal pain, diarrhoea, and significant weight loss. Current treatments primarily suppress the immune system — but fail to directly promote healing of the intestinal lining, which is the key predictor of long-term remission.
